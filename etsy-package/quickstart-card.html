Bitmoji Lab

Paint emoji art and send it to a NeoPixel LED matrix on the BBC micro:bit V2
Paint • Glow • Share • No install • 100% local
1
🔌

Wire the LED Matrix

Connect the NeoPixel matrix to the micro:bit: VCC→3V/ext power, GND→GND, DIN→P0. Use external power for 64 or 256 LEDs.

2
🤖

Flash the Receiver

Open makecode.microbit.org, switch to JavaScript, paste makecode.ts, hit Download and drag the .hex onto your micro:bit.

3
🌐

Open the App

Double-click index.html in Chrome or Edge. No server, no install — pure HTML + JS.

4
📡

Connect over USB

Click Connect, pick BBC micro:bit CMSIS-DAP. The board shows a ✅ when ready. Choose 8×8 or 16×16 from the Matrix Size dropdown.

5
🎨

Paint, Glow, Share

Click an emoji from the library (100+ across faces, robots, vehicles, tools, symbols, flags, road signs) or open Magic Paintbrush to paint your own pixels. Click 🚀 SEND TO MICRO:BIT and the matrix lights up. Name it, hit 💾 Save, and it lives in My Saved Designs forever.

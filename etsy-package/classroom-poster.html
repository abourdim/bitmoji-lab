Bitmoji Lab • Classroom Guide

1
🔌

Wire Your LED Matrix

Plug the NeoPixel matrix into the micro:bit: VCC→3V, GND→GND, DIN→P0. For 64 or 256 LEDs, use an external power pack so the board stays happy.

2
🤖

Flash the Receiver Code

Open makecode.microbit.org, paste the included makecode.ts, click Download, and drag the .hex onto the micro:bit USB drive. A ✅ means it’s ready.

3
🌐

Open the App in Chrome

Double-click index.html. Click Connect, pick BBC micro:bit CMSIS-DAP, and the status dot turns green — you’re live over USB!

4
🎨

Pick, Paint, or Play a Demo

Click any of the 100+ emojis to preview it. Or open Magic Paintbrush, grab a color, and click-drag to paint your own pixels. Shift-click erases. Try a demo like Heart Beat or Rainbow Wave.

5
🚀

Send It, Save It, Share It

Press 🚀 SEND TO MICRO:BIT — the matrix lights up. Type a name and hit 💾 Save to keep it. Photograph your glowing art and share it with the class!

Every pixel you paint becomes light.

Your micro:bit is a tiny computer. Today, you are its artist.
